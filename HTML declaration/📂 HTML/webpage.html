Foundation

Daarul-Hikmot Wal-Irshad was founded july 1997 by the great scholar Fadeelatu-Sheikh Abdul-Ganiy Muh.Awwal Al-Edewiy. At Daarul-Hikmot we offer your wards a better and sound education in the Arabic and Islamic Knowledge. we at Daarul-Hikmot take proper care of your children and gives them outstanding knowledge in the Arabic and Islamic perspective.

Subjects being thought in the Modrasat

  1. Qiraahatul-Qur'an
  2. Al-hadith
  3. Al-fiqhu
  4. Tahfeezul-Qur'an
  5. Al-Lugat
  6. An-Nahwu
  7. As-Sorfu
  8. Al-Adab
  9. Al-khatu wal Imlaah
  10. Qur'an Mujawwad
  11. Talqeenul-Qur'an
All this subjects are being taught in the modrasat depending on your wards class and level
