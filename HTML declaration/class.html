Suratu-L-Infitor

1. Iza-samoohun fatorat 2. waizal-kawaakibun tatharat 3. wa izal-bihaaru fujjirat 4. wa izal-qubuuru buhthirat. 5. alimat nofsun ma qoddamot wa akharat. 6. yaa ayyuhal insaanu ma garraka birobbikal kareem. 7. allaziy khalaqoka fasawwaaka fahadalak. 8. fiy ayyi suratin maa shaaha rakkabak. 9. kallaa bal tukazzibuna bi deen 10. wa inna alaikum lahaafizeen. 11. kiromon kaatibeen.12. yahlamuna mo tafhaluun 13. innal abroora lafiy naheem 14. wa innnal fujjara lafiy jaheem 15. yaslaonaha yaoma deen 16. wamo hum aniha bigaa ibeen 17. wamoadroka ma yaomu deeen 18. thumo mo adraaaka mo yaomu deen 19. yaoma laa tamliku nofsun linofsin shayhan wal amru yaomohizin lillaah.

Suratu-l-Inshiqoq

1. izaa-samoohun shaqqat 2. wa azinat lirobbiha wa uqqot 3. waizal ardu muddat 4. wa alqot ma fiiha watakhallat 5. wa azinot lirobbiha wa uqqot 6. yaa ayyuhal insaaanu innaka kaadihun ila robbika kadhan famulaaqiihi 7. fa ammo Mon uutiya kitaabahu biyameenihi 8. fasaofa yuhaasabu hisaaban yaseeron 9. wayanqolibu ilaa ahlihi mosruura 10. wa ammo mon uutiya kitaabahu waraaha zohrihi 11. fasaofa yadhuu thubuura 12. wayaslaa saheeran 13. innahu kaan fiy ahlihi mosruura 14. innahu zonna anlaa yahuur 15. balaa inna robbahu kaana bihi boseera 16. falaa uqsimu bishafaq 17. wallaeli wamo wasaq 18. wal qomori iza tasaq 19. latarkabunna tobaqon an tobaq 20. famaa lahum laa yuhminuun 21. wa izaa quriha alayhim Al-Qur'anu laa yasjuduun 22. balillazeena kafaruu yukazzibun 23. wallahu ahlamu bimo yuuhun 24. fabasbhirhum bi azaabin aleem 25. illalazeena amonu wa amilu solihaat lahum ajrun gaeru momnuun.
